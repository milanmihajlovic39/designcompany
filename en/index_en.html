About us

The Laboratory for Experimental Psychology in Novi Sad was founded in 1994 and is the part of the Department of Psychology. At the same time, it constitutes a research entity with the Laboratory for Experimental Psychology at the Faculty of Philosophy in Belgrade .

Our team

Research in our lab is currently conducted by nine faculty members, six PhD students and several undergraduate and MA students (graduates).

Experiments

Research in our lab is based on psychophysics, behavioral methods, various methods of quantitative psycholinguistics, eye-tracking, and ERP and EEG methods.

Ongoing research

In the Laboratory for Experimental Psychology, we conduct research in the fields:

  • Perception of colors and light
  • Lightness and multiple illumination levels
  • Perception of colors and language
  • Mental imagery
  • Face perception
  • Visual search and attention
  • Kinetic depth effect

Previous lines of research referred to the perceptual richness of words, as well as the processing and representation of ambiguous words.
